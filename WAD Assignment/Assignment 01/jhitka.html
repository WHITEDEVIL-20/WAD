Welcome to Jhitka

Jhitka is a small yet historically and culturally rich area located in Harirampur Upazila of Manikganj District, Bangladesh. Nestled along the banks of the Padma River, it is known for its scenic beauty, fertile agricultural land, and traditional lifestyle. The region has a deep connection to Bangladesh’s rural heritage, with agriculture being the backbone of the local economy.Over the years, river erosion has posed challenges to the community, yet the resilience of the people continues to shape the town’s identity.

Historical Places

  1. Kali Mondir (Ancient Hindu Temple)
  2. Padma River Bank (Historical Trade Route)
  3. Jhitka Bazar (One of the Oldest Markets)
  4. Harirampur Zamindar Bari
